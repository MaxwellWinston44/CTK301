COURSE REQUIREMENTS

Admission and Retention Requirements:

All students must undergo a portfolio review and/or an interview with the Creative Technologies Admissions Committee before being admitted into the program.
The Committee will evaluate admission requests from new beginning freshman, new transfer students, and internal transfers. The Creative Technologies program reserves the right to maintain a balanced enrollment and the number of students wishing to enter the Creative Technologies program may be restricted.
Students will be allowed to continue in the Creative Technologies program who have met and continue to maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and have demonstrated competency through an intermediate portfolio review.
Select from the following courses after completion of the Fine Arts Core, the Creative Technologies Core, and the Creative Technologies intermediate portfolio review.

  • 3 hours from the following: CTK 240, 300, 340, 345, or 380.
  • 3 hours from the following: CTK 300, ART 371, THE 360 with graphic topics, or CTK 380.
  • 9 hours from any of the following courses, or other courses that emphasize Creative Technologies applications with approval of the program advisor: CTK 260, 300, 320, 340, 345, 380, 390; ART 371
    or THE 360; ART 253, 351.24, 351.85;
    or THE 367; (multiple enrollments are allowed in CTK 300 and 380 may be repeated with different topics or experiences).
  • Capstone Experience in Creative Technologies (3 hours)
  • Internship in Creative Technologies (CTK 398 or comparable culminating experience in multimedia design as approved by the program advisor e.g. CTK 260).
